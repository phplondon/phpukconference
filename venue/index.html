PHP UK Conference is back at The Brewery this year, right in the heart of the City of London. The Brewery occupies the site of the former Whitbread brewery in East London. Samuel Whitbread opened The Brewery in 1750. Brewing on the site continued until 1976.

The Brewery is a beautiful venue, with unique rooms and ambient lighting which provide the perfect creative atmosphere, encouraging networking and learning. There will be dedicated space for delegates to relax, charge their equipment and network with other attendees.

The centre of the conference will be the 544 square metre exhibition and catering space. Buzzing with speakers, sponsors and delegates in this room you'll find all your refreshments, lunch and great info and swag from our awesome sponsors.

Ristretto will once again be providing coffee and espresso service during the event, much to the delight of all. This year we have also hired an extra coffee bar to ensure that you can recharge and be on your way quickly.

The whole venue is equipped with award winning wifi free for all attendees. Each talk room contains state of the art audio and video equipment so you know you'll get the best possible experience out of all the talks, including in the recorded video after the event.

The Brewery is fully handicap accessible and staff are available on site for assistance.

Accommodation

We have visited and inspected a number of hotels in the direct vicinity of the Brewery - we looked at rooms in each one and have selected three that we believe represent excellent value for conference accommodation. There are many other hotels in the area including, of course, the Montcalm which is located in the conference centre.

Other local hotels are shown on the Brewery site here.
