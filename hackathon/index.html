Hackathon

Collaborative programming contest presented by Nexmo

The Nexmo Hackathon is a fun, collaborative programming challenge you can take part in during the conference. The theme will be announced at the start of the conference and you'll be invited to build a project over the next day and half. You'll present and demo your app to a panel of judges who will choose a winning team or individual to receive a prize!

Challenge

Full challenge details will be revealed at the start of the hackathon. We can give you one clue: you'll build a project that uses Nexmo's Developer API's. You're especially encouraged to use Nexmo's Voice, Verify or Messages APIs.

Teams

You can work on the challenge as an individual or a team of up to three. Teams of more than three are not permitted.

Rules

To be eligible to present and win a prize, you're required to use at least one of Nexmo's API's. You'll need to submit your project by 12.30pm on the Friday so you can demo it to the judges. Nexmo will publish a form so you can share your project details. Follow them on Twitter as they will be sharing information during the conference.
