Hackathon

Collaborative programming contest presented by Nexmo

The Nexmo Hackathon is a fun, collaborative programming challenge you can take part in during the conference. You'll be given a challenge to write an app in just a day and a half over the conference days. You'll present and demo your app to a panel of judges who will choose a winner to receive a prize!

Lounge

The hackathon will be centred around the Nexmo Hackathon Lounge. The lounge is filled with:

  • 🛋 lots of comfy sofas and bean bags chairs
  • 🔋 power strips so you can charge your equipment
  • ☕️ a free coffee bar with professional baristas from Ristretto serving locally roasted Alchemy coffee any way you like.
  • ✔️coke and red bull if you prefer your caffeine that way
  • 🍰 cakes and biscuits served throughout the day
  • 🍕 fresh hot pizza served at lunch on Thursday
  • 🙋wonderful friends from Nexmo who will be available all day to answer any questions you might have about Nexmo or how to use their API's in your hackathon app.

Challenge

Full challenge details will be revealed at the start of the hackathon. We can give you one clue: you'll build an app that uses Nexmo's Developer API's.

Teams

You can work on the challenge as an individual or a team of two. Teams of more than two are not permitted.

Rules

Your app is required to use at least 1 of Nexmo's API's. Your app will need to be functional by 12.30pm on the Friday so you can demo it to the judges.

Timeline

Friday February 22nd

Prizes

There will be some great prizes going to the hackathon winners!
